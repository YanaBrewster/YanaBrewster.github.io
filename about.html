Hello!
I'm Yana, a UX/UI (User Experience/User Interface) designer
and front-end web developer based in Wellington.

Along with my background experience in graphic design and publications, I am a motivated, creative problem-solver with a strong work ethic, and a fast learner of new programs. I am also responsible, reliable and accountable. My supervisors and team-mates often comment on my good attention to detail and my ability to consistently deliver a high standard of work, while also showing initiative.

I love the process of designing and creating websites. The satisfaction of seeing a webpage complete and code behaving properly (as I have programmed it to perform) is what I enjoy most about web design - along with learning more about creating user-friendly designs with code.

My caring nature and bicultural upbringing enable me to relate to and empathise with a diverse range of people from different backgrounds and cultures.

Having a unique perspective allows me to sense other people's needs and wants intuitively and this drives me to find ways to solve their problems. Having a flair for design strengthens my ability to answer the user’s problem visually, in a user-friendly and effective way.

My overall goal is to support organisations that encourage positive social change, health and well-being in our community and I believe UX/UI design and front-end web development is a crucial tool and platform to raise awareness and solve problems in today's technological world.

For this reason, I am also very interested in website accessibility and am keen to learn more about it and incorporate it in future case studies.

In my next role, I want to build on my current skill set by refining my user experience knowledge and technique, or sharpen my coding skills and learn new coding languages.

I believe my personality and skill-set match the desirable characteristics of a UX/UI designer and front-end web developer, and that I will be a valuable asset to any team.
