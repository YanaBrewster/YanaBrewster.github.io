Artful

User research for an art selling and buying application

Artful is an app where NZ artists can sell their artworks in a variety of media to app users. The problems buyers faced were: 1) They did not know where to find the style of art they liked; 2) They want a personable experience buying art from the artist.

The requirements for this app were for users to buy and sell artwork. The seller can post a product with its associated details. Buyers are able to view, leave comments and ask questions about the product/s.

Hayley, Rahul and I worked together as a team to create the Artful app.
We had one month to complete the proposal, UX, design and coding process.
