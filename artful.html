Artful

For our group Yoobee assignment we were tasked to develop a fullstack app using Agile methodology, Node.js, npm, Grunt and MongoDB. The requirements for this app were for users to buy and sell their products. The seller can post a product with its associated details. Buyers are able to view, leave comments and ask questions about the product/s.

Hayley, Rahul and I worked together as a team to create the Artful app.
We had one month to complete the proposal, UX, design and coding process during Level 4 lockdown.

Discovery

Using Google Forms, two surveys were created for prospective sellers and buyers. After getting the survey proof-read, we started posting it on social media. Some highlights were that all survey takers are from New Zealand and 57.1% of them wanted to buy directly from the artists.

You can view the Buyer survey here and view the Seller survey here.

Analysis

We created a class diagram for the schemas required. These three schemas were coded for our database on MongoDB. Hayley and I collaborated on the type of content needed, taking our user research findings into consideration.

User Testing

There were three types of usability testing completed on UXPin (and the live site) across mobile and desktop devices. The testing methods were Remote, Beta and Cross Browser Testing. Beta Testing results confirmed that the design is user-friendly. The testing standards were: 1) They should complete the task in 4 clicks or less, excluding submit buttons. 2) Each task should take less than 5 minutes.
