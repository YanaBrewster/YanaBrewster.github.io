Graphic Design & Illustration

Posters

Eyegum Music Collective, 2015 & Eyegum Music Collective, 2016

The brief was to create a poster for a music gig. This vector images were drawn on Adobe Illustrator CS6, and coloured on Adobe Photoshop CS6. I also created a Facebook event cover image. The posters were advertised on facebook events and one was exhibited at a fundraising art event.

Vicki Eats & Te Aro Wine, 2019

This poster was created for a Vicki Eats and Te Aro Wine event. It was featured on their facebook and instagram pages to advertise the event.

www.facebook.com/vickieats

Vicki Eats & Baron Hasselhoffs, 2019

This poster was created for a cake auction where all proceeds went to Pink Ribbon NZ. The auction was a success, and Vicki Eats and Baron Hasselhoff’s raised $159 in total. The poster was featured on their facebook and instagram pages to advertise the event.

www.facebook.com/vickieats
www.facebook.com/BaronHasselhoffs

Vicki Eats & Baron Hasselhoffs, 2017

This poster was created for a cake auction where all proceeds went to the Wellington Women’s Refuge. The auction was a success, and Vicki Eats and Baron Hasselhoff’s raised $441 in total. The poster was featured on their facebook and instagram pages and prints were also made to advertise the event.

www.facebook.com/vickieats

Film for Change Aotearoa Christmas Party, 2016

This invitation was created for Film for Changes Christmas party. I was also the main organiser of the volunteer appreciation event. The invite was created on Adobe Illustrator CS6, and distributed via meetup and email.

During my time at Film for Change Aotearoa I was their main in-house designer and was often tasked design briefs with tight deadlines. Designing posters for public and private events, certificates and processes.

www.filmforchange.org.nz

Logos

Brewster Photo, 2017

The brief was to rebrand Barney Brewster photography, and create a new style guide and logo. This vector image was created on Adobe Illustrator CS6. The logo has since been featured on his website and publications.

www.brewster.co.nz

Riveted Raven Designs, 2016

The brief was to create a steampunk logo for the Riveted Raven Designs online jewellery shop. This vector image was drawn on Adobe Illustrator CS6, and coloured on Adobe Photoshop CS6. The logo was created solely for advertisement use on facebook and Etsy.

Chinoiserie Logo, 2014

The brief was to create a logo for the Chinoiserie cafe, located in Mt Albert, Auckland. This vector image was rendered on Adobe Illustrator CS6. How the logo has been utilised: As the brand’s mascot on social media, featured on their cafe menu, a lightbox sign for the shop front.
