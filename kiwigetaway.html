Kiwi Getaway Case Study

Accommodation App

The Yoobee assignment was to create a single-page web app for booking accommodation through Tourism New Zealand. The app allows users to input their information, display it as entered and show if validation has failed or passed. Users wanted to see accommodation ratings, pictures of the rooms and the cost of accommodation when making a booking.

We had 3 weeks to complete the proposal, user experience, design and coding process.
