Kiwi Getaway Case Study

Accommodation App

The Yoobee assignment was to create a single-page web app for booking accommodation through Tourism New Zealand. The app should allow users to input their information, display it as entered and show whether validation has failed or passed.

Tourism New Zealand does not yet have an app that supports their current campaign. Without the app, the campaign goal of attracting short-term travellers cannot succeed, as visitors cannot easily find this information.

We had 3 weeks to complete the proposal, UX, design and coding process. Here is a brief overview of the creative process. You can also view and download an extensive run-through of this case study too.

Strategy

For this activity, possible issues were outlined for users booking accommodation online.

After identifying these issues, options were explored for getting more guests to book short stays in NZ.

Discovery

An online booking accommodation survey was created using Google Forms and 46 responses were received. Interviews and a competitor analysis were two other research techniques used.

Analysis

Interview and survey responses were written on post-it notes (one per person) and placed into categories. I used the process of elimination to find the target audience.

Analysis of the survey and interview data confirmed that the target audience for the New Zealand accommodation booking website were: Women (70%); tourists between the ages of 21-30yrs old (68.42%) and on a low income under $25k annually (69.23%).

Design

The style guide was created based on Tourism New Zealand's colors and fonts. I selected a Google sans serif font alternative which loads faster and chose web-safe versions of the colours.

Initial sketches were done to jump-start the design process. Desktop, tablet and mobile versions were drawn up quickly, here is an example of the homepage and pop up module pages.

User Testing

Usability Testing (low, medium and high fidelity prototypes) was completed on InVision across the 3 viewing platforms: Desktop, mobile and tablet. Prototypes were created using Sketch and Adobe XD. Beta Testing on the final coded app was also completed to spot any bugs in JavaScript of which there were none.

Front-end Development

A use case was created to outline the function of booking accommodation.

Coding style guides were created to ensure consistent coding practises
