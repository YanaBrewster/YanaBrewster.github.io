Restaurant Case Study

For this assignment we had to work in groups. Our task was to redesign a website for responsive viewing on desktop, tablet and mobile. We chose to redesign the website for this brief through the process of elimination in our research, as the restaurants current website was not responsive, aesthetically appealing nor user friendly. Below is an overview of the UX process.

We had one week to complete the UX process together
and one week to code the website individually.

Timeline

As a group we had one week to complete the User Research and design process. This included collecting data, ideation, analysis, designing prototypes and user testing.

As individuals we had another week to complete the coding process in HTML5/CSS3.

Ideation

For our persona, we wanted her to be realistic. I was in charge of this activity and thought about how Linda (our persona) would earn a higher salary.

User Testing

Our group considered what our testing standards would be and what would be considered a pass for user testing. We then tested our prototypes in InVision. Prototypes were created using Sketch and Adobe XD.
